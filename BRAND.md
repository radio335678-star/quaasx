## Visual

- Black canvas (`#0a0a0a`), silver/chrome type, grey secondary
- No purple, cream, terracotta, neon glow, or emoji chrome
- Dark is the default experience

## Claims

- Say **cite-first / grounded in classical text**
- Say **verified English** only for gold or audited translations
- Do not claim “entire tradition” until corpus tiers support it
